The Essentials

1. Toilets:   If it didn't come out of your body and is not single ply toilet paper, it doesn't belong in the toilets.

2. Laws:   Big Bond takes place on private land, but state and federal laws still apply. You will most likely pass a border patrol check point on your way to and from Big Bond. A bad interaction with law enforcement is a sure way to mess up your Big Bond experience. Read more about this in the Law Enforcement section of the survival guide.

3. Safety:   The Chihuahuan Desert is a remote and inhospitable place. You are responsible for your own safety and decisions. The nearest EMS response center is 8 minutes from the camp grounds; however, the nearest hospital is 1.5 hours away. Practice caution and make responsible decisions. Wear lights at night, bring a small first aid kit, and look out for each other.

4. Preparation:   The Big Bond camp grounds have bathrooms and outdoor showers. However, the water is not potable. You are responsible for bringing your own potable water and any other supplies you will need, including shelter and food. Bring 1.5 gallons of water per person per day. While you may be able to buy supplies in Terlingua, no supplies will be sold at Big Bond.

2. Wristbands:   All attendees need a valid wristband. A wristband grants you entry to and camping at Big Bond. As long as you are still wearing a valid wristband, you may come and go from the grounds as you please for the duration of Big Bond. All wristband sales are final and will be mailed out a few weeks prior to Big Bond 2024. All wristband holders are legally bound to the terms and conditions.

The Big Bond Ethos

A tenet is a specific principle, belief, or doctrine that is a fundamental element of a larger set of guiding principles. The Big Bond community subscribes to a collection of tenets.
Together these tenets make up the Big Bond Ethos.


Accountability

Members of the Big Bond community practice personal responsibility and accountability. We do our best to be both physically and mentally prepared for all outcomes. We recognize that individuals are accountable for their own actions and decisions.


Authentic Expression

We encourage the full expression of self without judgment of self nor each other. Without judgment or normal social constructs, we are free to be our authentic selves. Acts of authentic expression should always respect the rights and liberties of others.


Inclusion

Anyone may join this community.
Big Bond encourages actively welcoming and assisting the stranger.


Leave No Trace

We celebrate and respect the natural world wherever we are. We accept accountability for cleaning up after ourselves and strive to leave spaces better than we found them.


Life Is Now

Big Bond - as with life - is best experienced in the moment. This community tries to overcome obstacles - both real and imagined - that prevent us from living in the present moment.
