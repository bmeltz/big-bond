Bring everything you'll need. Here's a list to help you out.


CAMPSITE

You'll need somewhere to sleep! If your vehicle's seats fold down, throw down some blankets and a few pillows and you're set. A tent is another great option as long as you bring a sleeping bag and an inflatable sleeping pad. If camping really isn't your thing and you have friends and money to spend, RV rentals can be found at websites like outdorrsy.com and cruiseamerica.com. We don't recommend hammocks as the only good place to string one up is between two vehicles. If you are a light sleeper, it might be a good idea to bring earplugs, especially if you plan on sleeping through the sunrise sets. A folding chair or two are also nice to have.

Bring trash bags! Leave your campsite better than you found it.
