ABOUT BIG BOND

Big Bond is not a typical music festival. Big Bond takes place in the remote west Texas Chihuahuan Desert.Yes, there is music and camping, but more importantly Big Bond is a celebration of nature and community. The Big Bond community subscribes to a collection of tenets such as Personal Responsibility, Inclusion, and Leave No Trace, among others, which collectively are refered to as the Big Bond Ethos. This Ethos acts as a guide for the values and culture of the Big Bond community.

You are invited to join this community.
