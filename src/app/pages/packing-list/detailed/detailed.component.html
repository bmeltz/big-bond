DETAILED PACKING LIST

A Tent, RV, or Vehicle with fold flat seats will work great. Sleeping bag, pillow, and any other comfort essentials. Suggested: yoga mat, sleeping pad, chairs, and canopy

Recommended: 1 gallon of water per person per day at a minimum. Plan your meals. Recommended: hotdogs (don't forget skewers), sandwiches, and snacks. Recommend at least 1 cooler with ice, paper towels, foil, utensils, and firewood. Exercise caution working with camping stoves and campfires.

It will be nice in the daytime and cool at night. (Ranging 40 - 75 degrees) Bring good tennis/hiking shoes, sunscreen. Festival attire is encouraged, but feel free to wear anything you like.

Towel, bathroom essentials, first aid kit, sunglasses, bug spray, trash bags, flashlight, backpack, some cash, chapstick, toilet paper, portable charger, and good vibes.
