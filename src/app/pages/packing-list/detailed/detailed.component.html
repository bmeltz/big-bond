DETAILED PACKING LIST

A Tent, RV, or Vehicle with fold flat seats will work great. Sleeping bag, pillow, and any other comfort essentials. Suggested: yoga mat, sleeping pad, chairs, and canopy

Recommended: 1.5 gallons of water per person per day at a minimum. It might seem like a lot, but water is one thing you definitely don't want to be worring about.
Plan your meals. Recommended: hotdogs (don't forget skewers), sandwiches, and snacks. Bring at least 1 cooler with ice, paper towels, foil, utensils, and firewood. Exercise caution working with camping stoves and campfires.

Bring good tennis/hiking shoes, sunscreen. Festival attire and costumes are encouraged, but feel free to wear anything you like. Practice authentic expression.

ear plugs are great. Towel, bathroom essentials, first aid kit, sunglasses, bug spray, trash bags, flashlight, backpack, some cash, chapstick, toilet paper, portable charger, and good vibes.
