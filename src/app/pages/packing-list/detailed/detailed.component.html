DETAILED PACKING LIST

A Tent, RV, or Vehicle with fold flat seats will work great. Sleeping bag, pillow, and any other comfort essentials. Suggested: yoga mat, sleeping pad, chairs, and canopy

Recommended: 1 gallon of water per person per day at a minimum. Plan your meals. Recommended: hotdogs (don't forget skewers), sandwiches, and snacks. Recommend at least 1 cooler with ice, paper towels, foil, utensils, and firewood.

It will be nice in the daytime and cool at night. (Ranging 40 - 75 degrees) Bring good tennis/hiking shoes, sunscreen. Feel free to wear anything you like, but festival attire is encouraged.

Towel, bathroom essentials, first aid kit, bug spray, trash bags, flashlight, backpack, some cash, chapstick, toilet paper, portable charger, and good vibes.
