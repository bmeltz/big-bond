Bring everything you will need.

There is no potable water at the campsite and none will be provided. Bring a minimum of 1 gallon per person per day.

Bring stuff to eat.

Bring your own shelter.

Bring trash bags! Pack out all of your trash. Leave no trace.

There are toilets and showers at the campsite.

Pack for 40-75 degree weather.

Be prepared. Be safe.
