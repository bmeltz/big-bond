What is this about leaving no trace? Are there not trash cans at Big Bond?

Leave No Trace is one of the tenets of Big Bond. You should read more about it in the Survival Guide, but basically you must not leave anything that you brought and if you see trash that isn't even yours, still pick it up! While there may be trash cans near the stage, these are strictly for your convenience when you finish a drink near the stage - not for that plastic packaging around that thing you just opened at your campsite.

Can I cook/grill at the campsite?

Yes! Small, portable propane stoves/grills are allowed for cooking. Please monitor your cooking devices and fully turn off butane tanks whenever you are not using them. Inhaling flammable gases is dangerous so keep these items away from enclosed spaces. Stay safe, and be aware of your surroundings while cooking. Campfire cooking is also allowed within designated fire pits. Never use gasoline to start a fire. Always clean up after yourself.

What comes with a campsite?

Campsites are included with your Big Bond wristband. The Big Bond campgrounds grounds have bathrooms and showers, but no potable water. Cars, RVs, Vans, and tent camping is allowed. Campsites are not assigned and you will pick your own spot. You can set up camp almost anywhere except in front of the stage. If you build a fire, please do so in one of the circles of rocks found at the campgrounds.

Will there be a general store at the campsites?

No, please bring all essentials. A cash only food truck may be available, but be prepared with all your own food. You are accountable for your own supplies. Sadly Uber eats will not make it out to the camp grounds but there is a gas station and a few restaurants within minutes.

Can I leave the campsite and come back?

Yep! as long as you are still wearing your valid Big Bond wristband you will not be asked to leave and you may come and go as you please.

Is it ok if I'm not planning on camping on the festival grounds?

Not a problem. However, it gets very dark at night, so driving around the festival grounds in the dark isn't allowed. There will be a designated parking area near the gate for attendees that aren't camping at the festival. You can come and go from this parking area as you please.

Will I be able to camp by my car?

You may park and set up camp almost anywhere in the grounds. Spots are given on a first come first serve basis and you pick your own spot. Please see the festival grounds map on the directions page.

Wherever your choose to set up camp, introduce yourself to the neighbors and include the stranger! Figure out if that is actually where you want to camp. Maybe the neighbors plan to party all night and you need sleep. Whatever the case may be, say hi and offer a hand to those around you.

Is there festival map?

There is a map of the Big Bond grounds on the directions page. Landmarks on the map are farther apart than they appear. Big Bond takes place on 640 acres of beautiful desert.

How does check-in work?

There are not assigned check-in times. Please already be wearing your wristband when you arrive. Big Bond volunteers will be waiting at the gate to check wristbands, get you checked-in, and welcome you to Big Bond.
